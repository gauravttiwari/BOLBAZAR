
# BolBazar Voice Commands Reference

This file lists all supported voice commands (Hindi + English) for the BolBazar project, organized by feature, with usage instructions.

---

## 🏠 Navigation
**How to use:**
Say any of these commands from any page to instantly navigate.
- "home", "go home", "मुख्य पृष्ठ" — Go to homepage
- "profile", "my profile", "account", "प्रोफाइल", "मेरा प्रोफाइल" — Open your profile page
- "logout", "log out", "sign out", "लॉगआउट", "लॉग आउट" — Log out of your account
- "track order", "order tracking", "my orders", "orders", "ऑर्डर ट्रैक", "मेरे ऑर्डर", "ऑर्डर" — Open order tracking page
- "open cart", "open card", "कार्ट खोलो" — Open your cart
- "close cart", "close card", "कार्ट बंद करो" — Close your cart

## 🔍 Search
**How to use:**
Say one of these commands followed by your search term to search for products.
- "search: shoes"
- "search for mobiles"
- "find: laptop"
- "खोजो: जूते"
- "सर्च: मोबाइल"

## 🛒 Cart
**How to use:**
Say these commands while viewing your cart or from any page.
- "clear cart", "कार्ट साफ करो" — Remove all items from cart
- "checkout", "check out", "चेकआउट" — Go to checkout page

## 📦 Checkout - Address Form
**How to use:**
While on the address form, say the field name and value to fill it by voice.
- "name: Ram", "नाम: राम"
- "mobile: 9876543210", "मोबाइल: 9876543210"
- "pincode: 110001", "पिनकोड: 110001"
- "state: Delhi", "राज्य: दिल्ली"
- "city: Mumbai", "शहर: मुंबई"
- "address: 123 Main Street", "पता: 123 मेन स्ट्रीट"
- "locality: Andheri", "इलाका: अंधेरी"
- "landmark: Near Park", "सीमाचिह्न: पार्क के पास"
- "address type home", "पता प्रकार घर"
- "address type work", "पता प्रकार कार्य"
- "address type other", "पता प्रकार अन्य"

## 💳 Checkout - Payment
**How to use:**
While on the payment step, say any of these to select Cash on Delivery.
- "cash on delivery", "cod", "nakad", "nakad par", "nakad par dijiye", "nakad chuno", "cash par dijiye", "cash select", "cod select", "cod chuno"

## 📝 Checkout - Place Order
**How to use:**
On the final step, say any of these to place your order by voice.
- "place order", "order now", "confirm order", "complete order", "ऑर्डर करो", "ऑर्डर कर दो", "ऑर्डर प्लेस करो", "ऑर्डर"

## ❌ Cancel Order
**How to use:**
While viewing your orders or order tracking, say any of these to cancel an order by voice.
- "cancel order", "order cancel", "ऑर्डर कैंसल करो", "ऑर्डर रद्द करो", "ऑर्डर हटाओ"

## 💬 Feedback / Help
**How to use:**
Say these commands to open the feedback form or get help:
- "give feedback", "feedback", "suggestion", "फीडबैक दो", "सुझाव दो"
- "help", "need help", "मदद करो", "सहायता चाहिए"

---

> All commands work in both Hindi and English where shown. For form fields, say the field name followed by the value. Use a clear and steady voice for best results.
